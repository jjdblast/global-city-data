Cities of the world

By admataz

My idea here is simplicity, and limiting the complex and diverse set of data to a view with single facets of the data that allow comparison.

I started this project as a tool-sharpening exercise. My goal was, after a recent hiatus from data visualisation work, to get to know some of the tools again, and get my head back into dealing with data - the processes of sourcing, cleaning, exploring, wrangling and transforming into something visual and interactive. You can read more and download the full source on github.

I found this collection of city data at the Greater London Authority London Datastore. For a more complete explanation of the figures, their units of measurement, and what they mean refer to the original New York City Global City database and the cited sources. For my purposes, this collection of numbers was enough for this experiment - but a better annotated and labeled version would be required to make this a full piece of data-driven communication.
